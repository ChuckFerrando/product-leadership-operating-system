# Playbook Naming Standard

---

# Format

[SCENARIO]_PLAYBOOK.md

---

# Rules

- uppercase  
- underscore separated  
- scenario-based  
- no vague terms  

---

# Good Examples

- RELEASE_READINESS_PLAYBOOK.md  
- PORTFOLIO_REVIEW_PREPARATION_PLAYBOOK.md  
- INITIATIVE_LIFECYCLE_PLAYBOOK.md  

---

# Anti-Patterns

- playbook.md  
- framework.md  
- model.md
