# Investment Memo Example  
## Initiative: AI-Assisted Portfolio Risk Analysis

### Executive Summary
Introduce an AI-assisted model to identify delivery risk across product initiatives earlier in the portfolio lifecycle.

The objective is to improve executive visibility into potential schedule, dependency, and resource risks before they materially impact delivery.

---

### Strategic Alignment
Supports enterprise goals of:

- improving delivery predictability  
- increasing decision velocity  
- enhancing portfolio transparency

---

### Investment Profile

| Category | Assessment |
|--------|--------|
| Strategic Alignment | High |
| Portfolio Priority | Tier 1 |
| Delivery Complexity | Medium |
| Organizational Impact | Medium |

---

### Risk Assessment

| Risk Category | Assessment |
|-------------|------------|
| Technical Risk | Medium |
| Organizational Risk | Low |
| Dependency Risk | Medium |
| Delivery Risk | Medium |

Mitigation: phased rollout with pilot initiatives.

---

### Expected Impact

- Earlier identification of delivery risk
- Improved executive portfolio visibility
- Faster intervention on high-risk initiatives

---

### Executive Recommendation

Proceed with a **pilot implementation across three portfolio initiatives** and review results during the next quarterly portfolio review.
