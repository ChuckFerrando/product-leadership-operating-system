# Investment Memo

This memo provides the structured decision brief used to evaluate portfolio investments.

All initiatives seeking capital allocation must submit an investment memo.

---

**Initiative Name:**  
**Sponsor:**  
**Decision Requested:** Approve / Reject / Revise

**Summary**

Provide a concise description of the proposed investment, the problem it addresses, and the expected strategic or mission impact.

---

# II. Strategic Alignment

Identify the enterprise or mission priorities advanced by this initiative.

- Strategic Priority Supported
- Capability Gap Addressed
- Expected Competitive / Mission Advantage

---

# III. Problem Statement

Describe the operational constraint or opportunity.

Include:

- Current limitations
- Operational or business impact
- Evidence supporting the need for intervention

---

# IV. Proposed Solution

Describe the proposed initiative.

Include:

- Delivery approach
- System or product components affected
- Implementation scope
