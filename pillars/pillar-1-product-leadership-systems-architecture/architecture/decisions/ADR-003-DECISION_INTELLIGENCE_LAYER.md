# ADR-003 — Decision Intelligence as a Cross-Cutting System

Status: Accepted  
Date: 2026-03-11

---

# Context

Product leadership decisions require reliable information, analytics, and learning feedback.

Traditional product operating models often treat analytics as a supporting function rather than an integrated leadership capability.

To enable better governance and strategic decision making, the architecture required a structured approach to intelligence and decision support.

---

# Decision

Introduce **Decision Intelligence** as a dedicated system within the Product Leadership Systems Architecture.

Decision Intelligence operates as a **cross-cutting capability** that supports all other systems.

It provides:

• analytics and performance insight  
• decision support models  
• outcome measurement  
• feedback loops for strategic learning  

---

# Rationale

Positioning Decision Intelligence as a system ensures that leadership decisions are informed by structured evidence rather than intuition alone.

It also ensures that data and learning flow across the entire architecture rather than being isolated within individual teams.

Decision Intelligence strengthens the leadership operating loop:

Strategy → Governance → Delivery → Outcomes → Learning.

---

# Consequences

Benefits include:

• stronger decision support  
• improved outcome measurement  
• faster organizational learning  
• better governance insight  

Constraints include:

• analytics capabilities must support multiple leadership systems  
• organizations must invest in reliable measurement frameworks
