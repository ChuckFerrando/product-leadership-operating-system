# Playbook Template

This template defines the canonical structure for all playbooks within the Product Leadership Operating System (PLOS).

---

# Core Principle

> Playbooks define how to act — not what to decide, what to measure, or what is valuable

All playbooks must preserve the canonical mediation path:

> Decision Intelligence System → Customer Outcomes System → Strategy / Governance

---

# Structure

## 1. Playbook Name
`[SCENARIO]_PLAYBOOK`

---

## 2. Context
Describe:
- when this playbook applies  
- trigger conditions  
- systems involved  

Do NOT:
- define strategy  
- interpret outcomes  
- introduce decision logic  

---

## 3. Objective
Define execution goal (not value or success criteria).

---

## 4. Inputs
List inputs from:
- Strategy  
- Governance  
- Delivery  
- Customer Outcomes System  
- Decision Intelligence System (signals only)

---

## 5. Execution Steps
- ordered  
- action-oriented  
- no decisions or interpretation  

---

## 6. Outputs
- execution artifacts  
- coordination outcomes  
- must flow correctly in PLOS loop  

---

## 7. System Interactions
Explicitly define interaction with:
- Strategy  
- Governance  
- Delivery  
- Customer Outcomes System  
- Decision Intelligence System  

---

## 8. Guardrails
Must include:
- no decisions  
- no interpretation  
- no outcome evaluation  
- no new pathways  

---

## 9. Failure Modes
List misuse scenarios and breakdowns.

---

## 10. Signals of Execution Health

**Critical Rule:**
- may reference signals  
- must NOT interpret, evaluate, or assign meaning
