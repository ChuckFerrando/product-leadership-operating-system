# Mediation Path Rule

All decision influence in PLOS must follow:

> Decision Intelligence → Customer Outcomes → Strategy / Governance

## Prohibited Paths

- Decision Intelligence → Strategy
- Decision Intelligence → Governance

## Interpretation Rule

Decision Intelligence may produce signals, metrics, dashboards, and descriptive analytics.

It may not:
- interpret
- evaluate
- assign value
- recommend
- decide

Customer Outcomes owns interpretation, evaluation, and learning.

Strategy and Governance own decision-making in their respective domains.
